LUXURY SPA AT THE UPPERMOST HOTEL JUMEIRAH

Welcome to “Senses Spa”, Jumeirah’s authentic Oriental spa experience. Let our Balinese and Thai therapists help re-align and balance your energies during your stay at The Uppermost, Jumairah. Experience a rejuvenating pampering session with a range of treatments offered here in a premium, luxury spa in Jumairah.

Signature Massages

Ensure your every need is catered to with our signature journey. Start with a full-body exfoliation followed by a nourishing hair and body mask. Allow your mind to relax while the frangipani scent fills the air, then feel all your tensions release as you enjoy a full-body massage and a fresh facial.

Ayurveda Massages

Ayurvedic massage, or Abhyanga, is a traditional Indian massage therapy that uses warm herbal oils and specific techniques to detoxify, balance energy, and rejuvenate the body, mind, and spirit. It's a holistic approach to healing that focuses on the interconnectedness of these three aspects.

Hot Stone Massages

Hot stone massage is a type of massage therapy that uses smooth, heated stones, typically basalt, to relax muscles, relieve pain, and improve circulation, often combined with traditional massage techniques.

Senses Facial Treatments

A "senses facial massage" is a type of facial massage that aims to enhance relaxation and well-being through a sensory experience, often incorporating aromatherapy and gentle techniques to soothe the face, neck, and upper shoulders.
